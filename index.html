About us

TequioNetz is a non-profit project that seeks to publicize the panorama of the various associations organized by Latin Americans that operate in Germany, we want to promote the visibility of these associations. We seek to offer a platform to provide an understandable and easily accessible catalog to Latino immigrants in Germany and thus facilitate participation in the associations of their choice.
